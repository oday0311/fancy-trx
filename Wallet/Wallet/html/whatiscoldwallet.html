What is a cold purse and a hot purse?

Cold purse (off line purse)

The wallet used in the absence of networking is a cold purse. For example: professional hardware wallet, the private key (or auxiliary word) is written on paper, and some people make use of equipment not connected to the Internet to make cold purse.

Advantages

  • Very safe

Shortcomings

  • It is inconvenient to create a purse
  • The deal is also very troublesome

 

Hot purse (online wallet)

The wallet that we used to connect all the time is all hot purse. For example: computer client wallet, mobile phone APP wallet, web wallet and so on.

Advantages

  • Easy to use, the novice is easy to operate
  • The efficiency of transaction transfer is relatively high

Shortcomings

  • Safety is not as good as cold Purse

 

Raybo Tips

Closing the mobile network and data traffic, or opening the flight mode directly, RayboWallet's hot wallet switches to a cold wallet. Instead, turn off the flight mode and open the network or data flow. The mobile phone connects to the network, the RayboWallet's cold purse will switch into a hot wallet, and the cold purse is safer.
